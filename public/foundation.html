Why give

  • Support a transportation-focused nonprofit mission.
  • Help expand reliable ride access for people in need.
  • Strengthen community support and outreach efforts.
  • Help remove mobility barriers that limit daily opportunity.

Why it matters

Transportation is often the missing link between people and opportunity. Access to a ride can help someone reach care, keep a job, stay in school, and remain connected to resources that support daily life and long-term stability.

Supporter FAQ

Clear answers for supporters, partners, and community members exploring the foundation mission.

What does the foundation support? Harvey Transportation Assistance Foundation supports transportation access tied to medical appointments, school, work, and essential community mobility.
How do I donate? Use the secure donation button or direct Stripe donation link on this page to contribute.
Can I share this page with supporters? Yes. This page is designed to be shared with supporters, partners, and community contacts who want to help.
Is this separate from Harvey Taxi Service LLC? Yes. The foundation mission should remain clearly separated from the for-profit ride platform while both can support the broader transportation-access ecosystem.
