Harvey Taxi Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026

Harvey Taxi Service LLC ("Harvey Taxi", "we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Harvey Taxi mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Pickup and drop-off address
  • Driver and rider location during trips
  • Device information
  • Usage data

How We Use Information

  • Provide ride requests
  • Match riders with drivers
  • Enable trip tracking
  • Improve dispatch performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Enhance platform safety

Location Data

Harvey Taxi collects location data to enable ride requests, driver dispatch, and trip tracking. Location data is only collected while using the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only between riders and drivers to complete trips.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

Harvey Taxi may use third-party services for identity verification, background checks, and payments.

Your Privacy Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
