Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Harvey Taxi respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Harvey Taxi Service LLC collects, uses, stores, and protects information when riders, drivers, merchants, and visitors use the Harvey Taxi platform.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, and account details you provide during signup.
  • Ride request details such as pickup address, dropoff address, ride status, and trip timing.
  • Driver onboarding information, which may include identity verification and background screening data handled through approved third-party providers.
  • Payment-related information used to process transactions through secure payment partners.
  • Device, browser, and usage information needed to improve performance, security, and support.
  • Location-related information when needed to support ride functionality, dispatch, ETA, navigation, and safety features.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage rider, driver, and merchant accounts.
  • To process ride requests and help connect riders with available drivers or fleet services.
  • To support identity verification, safety review, fraud prevention, and platform security.
  • To communicate service updates, ride details, support responses, and important notices.
  • To improve dispatch, route coordination, user experience, and platform operations.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

Sharing of Information

Harvey Taxi may share limited information with service providers and partners that help operate the platform, including verification providers, background screening providers, mapping providers, hosting providers, analytics tools, payment processors, and customer support tools. We may also share information when required by law, to protect safety, or to investigate misuse of the platform.

Data Security

Harvey Taxi uses reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards to help protect user data. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You may contact us to request account updates or corrections.
  • You may choose not to provide certain information, though some features may not function properly.
  • You may discontinue use of the platform at any time.

Children’s Privacy

Harvey Taxi is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the platform.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact Harvey Taxi Service LLC through the support information provided on the platform or official business channels.

This page is provided for general platform transparency and review readiness. You should later match this policy exactly to your final production flows, providers, and legal business contact details.
