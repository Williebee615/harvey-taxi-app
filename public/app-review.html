Platform Purpose

Harvey Taxi is a transportation platform designed for rider requests, driver onboarding, dispatch coordination, and platform management.

Test Guidance

Reviewers should be able to access the rider signup, driver signup, request ride, support, privacy policy, and terms of service pages from the main experience.

Important Note

Final reviewer credentials and special testing notes should also be supplied directly inside App Store Connect during submission.

Current Build Focus

This build emphasizes transportation workflow visibility, service legitimacy, support access, and transparent policy pages while the platform continues moving toward full production rollout.
