Main User Paths

These are the primary consumer-facing flows shown within the app.

👤
Rider Account Flow Users can open rider signup, complete onboarding steps, and move toward ride access.
🚖
Driver Application Flow Drivers can apply, complete onboarding requirements, and await approval.
📍
Ride Request Flow Approved rider access can proceed into the trip request process.

Rider Flow Summary

A simple summary of how rider-side access works.

Step 1 — Account Entry User opens the rider signup page and enters account information.
Step 2 — Review / Verification User completes required rider onboarding steps before full ride access is granted.
Step 3 — Ride Request Access Once approved, the rider can proceed into the ride request flow.

Driver Flow Summary

A simple summary of how driver-side access works.

Step 1 — Driver Sign Up User opens the driver signup page and submits onboarding information.
Step 2 — Driver Review Driver onboarding includes review and approval requirements before driver activation.
Step 3 — Platform Participation Approved drivers can move toward platform participation and trip-related workflow.
