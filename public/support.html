Support Guidance

Clear guidance helps make the product feel more official and understandable.

  • Riders may be required to complete account steps before ride access is approved.
  • Drivers may be required to complete onboarding and approval before going live.
  • Support pages are provided to help users access policies and assistance information.
  • Ride request functionality is available through the Harvey Taxi request flow.
  • Payment access is provided through Harvey Taxi payment pages and related services.

For urgent safety matters, users should contact local emergency services when immediate help is needed.
