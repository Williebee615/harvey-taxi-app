How It Works

Harvey Taxi keeps rider flow, payment readiness, verification, and trip activity organized through a cleaner sequence.

1. Sign Up Create a rider account or apply as a driver through the Harvey Taxi platform.
2. Complete Access Flow Riders complete approval steps while drivers complete onboarding and qualification steps.
3. Request a Ride Approved riders can request a standard driver ride or view clearly labeled pilot flows.
4. Prepare Payment Payment readiness helps support cleaner dispatch and smoother trip operations.
5. Get Matched The platform routes the trip through dispatch and mission logic to the best available driver.
6. Ride with Support Track the trip, access help if needed, and manage the experience through one connected system.
