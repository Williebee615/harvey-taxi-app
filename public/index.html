Harvey Transportation Assistance Foundation Mission

Our mission is to help reduce transportation barriers for individuals and families in need by supporting access to essential rides, dependable mobility solutions, and future community transportation programs. We aim to expand transportation support locally and internationally while building a lasting connection between mobility, opportunity, and community care.

Medical Access Support transportation for appointments, treatment, and urgent mobility needs.
School & Work Help people reach school, employment, and opportunity more consistently.
Community Mobility Grow transportation support programs where dependable access is needed most.
