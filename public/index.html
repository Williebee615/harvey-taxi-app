How It Works

Simple steps for riders and drivers.

1. Sign Up Create a rider account or apply as a driver.
2. Get Approved Riders complete access checks and drivers complete onboarding before going live.
3. Request a Ride Approved riders can request a standard driver ride or view the autonomous pilot flow.
4. Authorize Payment Payment authorization helps keep dispatch and trip flow cleaner and more reliable.
5. Get Matched The platform routes the trip through dispatch and mission flow to the best available driver.
6. Ride with Confidence Track your ride, get support when needed, and manage the trip through one connected system.
