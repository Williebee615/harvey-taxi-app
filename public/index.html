What Harvey Taxi Is Building

Rider Platform

Riders can request trips, track ride progress, and move through a cleaner mobile-first transportation experience built for real everyday use.

Driver Platform

Drivers can sign up, get approved, accept assigned trips, manage trip flow, and work through a growing dispatch system designed for expansion.

Dispatch & Admin Control

Harvey Taxi includes admin visibility for rides, drivers, rider activity, trip status, approvals, assignments, and future live operational control.

Live Tracking Direction

The platform is being upgraded toward live driver mapping, rider visibility, ETA-style updates, and a stronger Uber-style trip lifecycle.

Need a ride now? Open the request screen and submit a live trip request into the Harvey Taxi system. Already requested a ride? Use your Ride ID to follow trip status updates and view driver progress. Driving for Harvey Taxi? Open the driver dashboard to manage assigned trips and live driver activity.
