Harvey Transportation Assistance Foundation Mission

Our mission is to help remove transportation barriers for individuals and families in need by supporting access to essential rides, dependable mobility solutions, and future community transportation programs. Through this work, we aim to expand transportation support locally and internationally while building a lasting bridge between mobility, opportunity, and community care.

Medical & Essential Access Support transportation for appointments, treatment, and urgent travel needs.
School & Work Mobility Help individuals reach school, work, and opportunity with greater consistency.
Community Growth Build long-term programs that expand transportation support where it is needed most.
