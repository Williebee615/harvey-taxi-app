How Harvey Taxi Works

A simple walkthrough for riders, drivers, and launch visitors.

1. Create Access Riders create access through rider signup. Drivers apply through a separate driver onboarding flow.
2. Approval & Readiness Rider approval and driver qualification help organize safer participation before live trip activity.
3. Choose Your Ride Approved riders can request a driver ride or enter the autonomous pilot flow where presented.
4. Payment Workflow Payment authorization supports cleaner dispatch, trip clarity, and stronger platform control.
5. Dispatch & Mission Flow Ride fulfillment is organized through structured trip handling and role-based platform flow.
6. Support & Oversight Support resources, policy access, and admin structure help improve accountability and service quality.
