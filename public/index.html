What Harvey Taxi Is Building

Rider Platform

Riders can create accounts, request trips, track ride activity, and access a cleaner mobile-first experience built for real-world transportation needs.

Driver Platform

Drivers can apply, upload documents, get reviewed by admin, and eventually receive live dispatch opportunities based on trip demand and location.

Dispatch System

Harvey Taxi is being built toward real dispatch flow with nearest-driver matching, trip acceptance, ride status updates, and better admin oversight.

Future Expansion

The platform is designed to grow into airport transportation, medical transport, grocery and restaurant delivery, and a larger multi-service marketplace.
